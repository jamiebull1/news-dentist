Getting round Google

  • drag this Get Cookie link to your bookmark bar before you start searching
  • if you are shown a CAPTCHA, fill it out and submit it
  • when taken to the results page, click the "Get Cookie" bookmark
  • copy the cookie value with Ctrl-C or Cmd-C from the message box that pops up (works on Chrome, not on Firefox, untested elsewhere).
  • return here and paste the cookie value into the cookie field of the advanced settings below.
